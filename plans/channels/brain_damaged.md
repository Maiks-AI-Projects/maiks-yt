# Brain Damaged (bd.maiks-yt.com)

## Vision
A chaotic, high-energy space for comedy, experiments, and unpredictable content.

## Core Features
- **Glitchy UI Effects**: Intentional UI glitches and visual distortions that fit the aesthetic.
- **Chaos Mode**: Interactive buttons that trigger random, unpredictable events on the stream.
- **Interactive Challenges**: High-energy viewer-driven challenges and tasks.
- **Media Gallery**: A space for viewers to upload and share chaotic media.

## Technical Goals
- High-contrast, vibrant, and intentional UI glitches.
- Robust, real-time communication via WebSockets.
- AI-assisted content filtering and safety checks.
