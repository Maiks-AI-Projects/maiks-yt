# AI (ai.maiks-yt.com)

## Vision
A futuristic hub for AI research, model training, and generative art.

## Core Features
- **Model Playground**: Interactive tools for testing and exploring AI models.
- **Generated Gallery**: A collection of AI-generated art and media.
- **Research Logs**: Detailed logs and findings from AI research projects.
- **AI-Assisted Tools**: Real-time tools for AI-driven creative workflows.

## Technical Goals
- Ethereal, neural network-inspired UI.
- Integration with major AI frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow).
- AI-assisted content tagging and verification.
