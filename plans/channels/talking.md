# Talking (talking.maiks-yt.com)

## Vision
A clean, conversation-focused space for general discussions, podcasts, and community hangouts.

## Core Features
- **Podcast Archive**: Easy access to previous live stream recordings and audio-only podcasts.
- **Polls and Q&A**: A live dashboard for viewers to submit and vote on questions during streams.
- **Community Wall**: A space for viewers to share thoughts and media that are filtered by AI.
- **Real-time Feedback**: Integration with overlays for instant visual feedback from the audience.

## Technical Goals
- Modern, minimalist, and highly readable UI.
- Secure, real-time communication via WebSockets.
