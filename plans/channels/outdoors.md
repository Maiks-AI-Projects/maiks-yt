# Outdoors (out.maiks-yt.com)

## Vision
A natural, Earth-toned hub for hiking, travel, and adventure.

## Core Features
- **Hiking Trail Logs**: Detailed records of trails hiked and findings.
- **Adventure Photo Gallery**: High-resolution photos from the creator's adventures.
- **Travel Vlogs**: Direct access to the creator's latest travel videos.
- **Hiking Gear List**: A collection of recommended gear and equipment.

## Technical Goals
- Natural tones (earthy greens/blues) and organic shapes.
- Integration with popular hiking and travel mapping tools.
- AI-assisted route planning and location tagging.
