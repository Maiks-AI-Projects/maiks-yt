# Legal, Tax, and Policy Pages

## Idea

Create legal/policy pages for the website and support system.

Possible pages:

- privacy policy
- terms of use
- donation/support terms
- refund/revocation explanation
- affiliate disclosure
- sponsor disclosure
- community rules
- abuse/reporting policy
- data retention policy

## Why It Matters

The project involves donations, affiliate income, sponsor money, credits, public withdrawals, private messages, user accounts, platform integrations, and public transparency.

Even if the streamer operates as a private person, the wording around support, refunds, credits, and public records should be clear.

## Current Assumption

The streamer currently expects to operate as a private person in the Netherlands and believes tax may not apply in the same way as a business.

This should still be verified before accepting money publicly, because donations, sponsorships, affiliate income, and recurring support may be treated differently.

## Build Requirements

- legal/policy page templates
- editable policy content
- version history for policy changes
- acceptance tracking if needed
- links from donation, profile, affiliate, sponsor, and account pages

## Open Questions

- Which policies are needed before version one goes live?
- Should users accept terms before donating?
- Should policy changes notify logged-in users?
- What wording is needed for credits and revocation?
