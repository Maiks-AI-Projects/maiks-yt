# Public Accountability and History Section

## Idea

Create a public section where the streamer can honestly explain difficult parts of their past, including criminal early years and failed businesses.

The goal is not to sensationalize the past, but to avoid hiding important context that could later be framed as a scandal if someone else brings it up first.

## Why It Matters

Being open gives the streamer control over the context and tone. It lets viewers hear the story directly instead of through rumors, screenshots, or hostile summaries.

It also supports trust. A community built around transparency should allow room for growth, mistakes, accountability, and changed behavior.

## Content Principles

- be factual
- avoid exaggeration or self-erasure
- acknowledge harm or mistakes where appropriate
- explain what changed
- avoid naming other private people unnecessarily
- avoid sharing sensitive legal or personal details that do not need to be public
- keep the tone accountable, not defensive
- make it easy to update if needed

## Data Needed

- public accountability/history content
- last updated time
- optional related links
- visibility settings
- moderation/admin edit history

## Build Requirements

- editable public page or section
- clear navigation from the broader personal context/about page
- ability to update without code changes
- optional short summary with a longer full version
- careful layout that feels human and serious, not dramatic

## Legal and Privacy Notes

This section should be written carefully. It may be worth getting trusted legal or personal review before publishing details about criminal history, failed businesses, other people, or anything that could create new risk.

Transparency does not require publishing every detail. The important part is owning the truth in a responsible way.

## Open Questions

- Should this be part of the main personal context page, or a separate linked page?
- How much detail is useful without becoming harmful?
- Should there be a short "what I want people to understand" summary?
- Should failed businesses be explained separately from criminal early years?
- Should this page include what boundaries are not up for public debate?
