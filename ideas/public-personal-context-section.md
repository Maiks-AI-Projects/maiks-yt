# Public Personal Context Section

## Idea

Create a public section on the website where the streamer can explain important personal context in their own words.

Topics may include:

- health issues
- how those health issues affect streaming
- difficult personal history the streamer chooses to disclose
- living next to an ex-wife
- having a son 50% of the time
- why streams may be irregular, shorter, cancelled, or lower energy
- how the community can be supportive without making it weird

## Why It Matters

The streamer is open about these parts of life and does not want to hide them. A clear public section can answer common questions, reduce awkward repeated explanations, and help viewers understand the human context around the stream.

It also supports healthier expectations. If a stream ends early, starts late, or gets cancelled, people already have context instead of assuming the worst.

## Data Needed

- public profile/about content
- health/context sections
- visibility settings
- last updated time
- optional related links
- optional stream schedule explanation

## Build Requirements

- public about/context page
- editable admin content
- ability to update text without deploying code
- clear navigation from profile/landing pages
- optional link from stream cancellation notices
- optional link to a public accountability/history section
- respectful layout that does not feel like a medical record

## UX Notes

The section should be direct and human, not hidden in tiny footer text. But it should also be controlled by the streamer and easy to update.

Being public about health does not mean every cancellation or stream moment needs to expose every detail. The system can support both openness and boundaries.

## Open Questions

- Should this live on the main profile page, a separate "About" page, or both?
- Should different landing pages show the same context or a shortened version?
- Should cancellation notices link to this page?
- Should accountability/history live here or on a separate linked page?
- Should there be a short version for overlays or social posts?
